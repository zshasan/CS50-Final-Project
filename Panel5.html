Chapter 5


Town Square

Our hero heads to the town square in search of information.

Looking around he notices how bustling and crowded the square is.

He then notices a group of merchants discussing something intriguing and heads towards them.

Town Square

They seem to be discussing an artifact in their possession.

Our hero learns of this ancient ruin filled with riches beyond your imagination.

For gold they offer to tell him of its location, but advise him to consider hiring a mercenary.

Our hero thanks them and makes his way out of the town noticing the mercenaries near the exit.
