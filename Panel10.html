Chapter 10


Finale

Our hero charges towards the treasure all while dodging all types of traps(arrows, spikes, etc.)

By moving in a zig-zag pattern he is able to keep an eye on the entire room and dodge what he needs to.

Finale

Using his sword he deflects arrows and projectiles.

Right when he reaches the the steps before the treasure, a giant spiked wrecking ball swings towards our hero.

Luckily by sacrificing his sword he deflects the wrecking ball and propels himself up the steps.

Finale

Our hero gets up and dusts himself off while looking back.

The traps deactivate and the room returns to what it looked like before.

Turning around our hero basks in the glory of his victory.
